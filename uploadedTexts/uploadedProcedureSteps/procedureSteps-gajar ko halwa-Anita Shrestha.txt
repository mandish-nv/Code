open gas and place carrot inside cooker put  6 pcs of carrot along with milk and water for about 15 mins then smash the carrot 
then after place pan and put ghee on pan then after put smash carrot in pan and fry carrot for some time until it became brownish (matlab dadauney hoina)
then put khuwa and put milk and let it be for some time and move slowly until it became dry gajar 
after obtaining awesome ready to gajar add some dry fruit for more flavour and now gajar is ready to eat
